Selected Projects
Lang-Grouping: Object-centric grouping for a better understanding of 3D scenes
3D vision course final project  ·  2024.04 – 2024.06
  • Object-level language–3D scene understanding framework, reducing inference time compared to LangSplat.
  • Propose an object-centric contrastive learning approach to enhance multi-view consistency for language feature distillation in Gaussian Splatting.
AnyCall: Video-Based Anomaly Detection for Industrial Display Device Inspection Using Context Alignment and Localizing Differences
Research project, On-going  ·  2024
  • Video-based anomaly detection system for industrial display inspection, capable of identifying defects during long-term multi-device operation.
  • Introduce a disparity synchronization module for frame-level alignment and a difference explorer module for robust, noise-resistant anomaly detection.
Gomoku AI: Demonstrating a Vision-Based AI Robot for Strategic Board Games
HRI course final project, Under review  ·  2024
  • Developed a low-cost human–robot interaction system for playing Gomoku, integrating real-time vision, AI-based decision-making, and robotic arm control.
  • Offers an affordable, real-world gaming experience that merges physical gameplay with intelligent decision-making.
AnyBald: Toward Realistic Diffusion-Based Hair Removal In-The-Wild
Research project, Under review  ·  2025
  • Developed AnyBald, a mask-free diffusion-based framework for realistic hair removal in the wild.
  • Achieves natural bald manipulation while preserving facial identity, enabling robust performance across diverse real-world scenarios.
